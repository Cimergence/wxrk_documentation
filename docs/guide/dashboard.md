# Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is your WXRK home screen. It gives you a quick overview of your activity and provides shortcuts to the most common actions.

Navigate to **Dashboard** from the left sidebar or click the WXRK logo.

---

## KPI cards

Four metric cards are shown at the top:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **CVs generated** | Total number of CV documents generated, with a trend indicator |
| **In progress** | Applications in `Draft` or `Generated` state |
| **Drafts** | Applications that have not yet been generated |
| **Success rate** | Percentage of applications that reached Interview or Offer stage |

---

## Activity feed

The activity feed shows a chronological log of recent actions:

- CV or cover letter generated
- Technical Review updated
- Application created
- Application status changed (e.g. moved to Interview)

Activity items are formatted as *action — role at company*, with a relative timestamp.

---

## Draft applications

If you have unsaved or un-generated draft applications, they appear in a **Drafts** panel with a quick link to open each one.

---

## Dashboard mode

The dashboard adapts based on your setup progress:

| Mode | Shown when |
|---|---|
| **Import CV** | No technical review experiences added yet — prompts you to import a CV |
| **Technical Review** | You have a technical review but no experiences — prompts you to add your first job |
| **Tools** | You have at least one experience — shows stats, activity, and shortcuts |

---

## Quick actions

From the dashboard you can:

- **+ New application** — jump directly to the new application form
- **Go to Technical Review** — if your review is empty or incomplete
- **Import CV** — upload an existing CV to auto-populate your Technical Review (see below)

---

## CV Import

WXRK can parse an existing CV (PDF or DOCX) to pre-populate your Technical Review.

1. Click **Import CV** on the Dashboard
2. Upload your CV file
3. WXRK extracts experiences, skills, and education
4. Review the extracted data in Technical Review and fill in any gaps

:::info LinkedIn import
You can also import from LinkedIn. Connect your LinkedIn account and WXRK will pull your experience data directly. This feature requires LinkedIn OAuth to be configured.
:::

---

## Greeting

The dashboard shows a time-of-day greeting:

- **Good morning** — before 12:00
- **Good afternoon** — 12:00–18:00
- **Good evening** — after 18:00

Your first name is pulled from your account profile.
