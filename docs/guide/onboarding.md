# Onboarding

When you first sign in to WXRK, you are guided through a **4-step onboarding wizard**. This takes about 3 minutes and collects the minimum information needed to start generating documents.

You can always update everything later from **Profile** and **Technical Review**.

---

## Step 1 — Welcome

The welcome screen introduces WXRK and explains what you'll set up. Click **Get started** to proceed.

---

## Step 2 — Your Profile

Fill in your basic details:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Full name** | Yes | Used on all generated documents |
| **Location** | No | City, Country format (e.g. *Zürich, Switzerland*) |
| **LinkedIn URL** | No | Included in the CV contact block |
| **Target role** | No | Your intended job title (e.g. *Senior Backend Engineer*) |
| **Career stage** | No | Actively applying / Exploring options / Changing careers |

Click **Continue** when done.

---

## Step 3 — Personality (MBTI)

WXRK uses your **MBTI personality type** to calibrate the tone and voice of your cover letters. There are two ways to set it:

### Option A — Take the mini MBTI test

Answer 8 questions on a Likert scale. Each question maps to one of the four MBTI dimensions:

| Dimension | Poles |
|---|---|
| E / I | Extraversion vs. Introversion |
| S / N | Sensing vs. iNtuition |
| T / F | Thinking vs. Feeling |
| J / P | Judging vs. Perceiving |

Your answers are scored and a four-letter type is computed automatically (e.g. **INTJ**, **ENFP**).

### Option B — Know your type already?

If you've taken a full MBTI assessment before, skip the test and select your type directly from the grid of 16 types organised into four groups:

- **Analysts** — INTJ, INTP, ENTJ, ENTP  
- **Diplomats** — INFJ, INFP, ENFJ, ENFP  
- **Sentinels** — ISTJ, ISFJ, ESTJ, ESFJ  
- **Explorers** — ISTP, ISFP, ESTP, ESFP  

:::info What does MBTI affect?
Your MBTI type influences the **adjective palette, sentence structure, and emphasis** of your cover letter. For example, an INTJ cover letter is *strategic and precise*, while an ENFP cover letter is *visionary and people-driven*.
:::

---

## Step 4 — Ready

A confirmation screen shows your MBTI type with its title and a personalised tone preview. You're taken to the Dashboard.

---

## Retaking or overriding your MBTI

You can change your MBTI type at any time from **Profile → Personality type**:

- **Retake test** — runs the 8-question quiz again
- **Override manually** — enable the toggle and pick any of the 16 types from a dropdown

Changes take effect on the next generated cover letter.
