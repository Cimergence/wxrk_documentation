# Profile

Your **Profile** is the foundation of everything WXRK generates. It stores who you are — your contact details and personality type — so the AI can write documents that sound like *you*.

Navigate to **Profile** from the left sidebar.

---

## Personal information

### Basic details

| Field | Used for |
|---|---|
| **Full name** | Header of every CV and cover letter |
| **Email** | Read-only — set via your login method |
| **Phone** | Contact block on generated CVs |
| **Location** | City / Country shown in CV header |

### Online presence

| Field | Used for |
|---|---|
| **LinkedIn URL** | Clickable link in CV contact block |

:::tip Add your GitHub
If you are a developer, your GitHub URL is stored in the system and can appear in CV contact blocks. Make sure to fill it in once the field is available in the UI.
:::

---

## Saving changes

An **unsaved changes banner** appears at the top of the page whenever you modify a field. You can either:

- **Save changes** — persists all edits to the server
- **Discard** — reverts to the last saved state

---

## Profile photo

Click on your avatar or the **Upload photo** button to attach a profile image.

- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum size: 5 MB
- The photo appears in the app UI only — it is not embedded in generated PDFs unless your CV template includes a photo block.

## Personality type (MBTI)

The right-hand card shows your current MBTI type and a breakdown of your four dimensions:

| Dimension | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **I / E** | How you recharge (Introversion vs. Extraversion) |
| **N / S** | How you process information (iNtuition vs. Sensing) |
| **T / F** | How you make decisions (Thinking vs. Feeling) |
| **J / P** | How you approach structure (Judging vs. Perceiving) |

Each bar shows the percentage score from your last test, from 0% (strongly left pole) to 100% (strongly right pole).

### Changing your type

| Action | When to use |
|---|---|
| **Retake MBTI test** | You want a fresh score from the 8-question quiz |
| **Override type manually** | You already know your type from an external test |

To override: click **Override type manually**, enable the toggle, then select your type from the dropdown. Save from the banner at the top.
